"The finest school of witchcraft and wizardry in the world."

  • Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry was the British wizarding school, located in the Scottish Highlands.
  • We accept magical students from Great Britain and Ireland for enrolment.
  • Our school is a state-owned school,funded by the Ministry of Magic.
  • Our School Established around the 10th century,also considered to be one of the finest magical institutions in the Wizarding World.
  • Children with magical abilities were enrolled at birth, and acceptance was confirmed by owl post at age eleven.
  • If the child in question was a Muggle-born, a staff member fromthe school visited the child and his or her family in order to inform them of their magical heritage and theexistence of the Wizarding world.
